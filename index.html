MY IDEAS

As a digital leader, I have many ideas on how to use technology to improve our school.
In addition to these ideas, I would also like to hear from the students of STC about their ideas for the school.

  • INSTANT TEACHER MESSAGING

    As we all know, teachers may not always check their emails and their inboxes are constantly flooded. If a student needs a teacher's urgent help and guidance, this system will work wonders.

  • ICT REPS IN EACH YEAR

    I believe that something that would benefit our school is to have ICT representatives for each year, helping all students in the year with any possible technical difficulties.

  • SUBJECT WEBSITES

    I believe a website for each subject where students can contribute notes, resources and knowledge to help each other would heavily benefit our education.

  • AUDIO NOTES

    Subject faculties could create audio notes for students, so when students are commuting they are able to revise and study in a convenient manner, improving education.

  • MENTAL WELLBEING APPLICATION

    It is common knowledge than IB and IGCSE are demanding for students. This app will ask students for their reflections and feelings each day, and will alert guidance counselors and teachers if there is cause for concern, protecting the mental wellbeing of students.

  • SCHOOL ROOM APPLICATION

    An application in which students can view a map of the school to prevent them from getting lost, see which room they are in for class and google maps-esque features showing them how to get there, and a booking system so teachers and students know which rooms are free in case they need one for extra-curricular activities.
